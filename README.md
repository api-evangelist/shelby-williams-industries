# Shelby Williams Industries (shelby-williams-industries)

Shelby Williams Industries is the premier designer, manufacturer, and distributor of seating products in the hospitality and food service industries. With over 70 years of experience, the company produces an extensive line of chairs, barstools, banquet seating, sofas, and tables made from wood, metal, rattan, and fully upholstered materials. Headquartered with US manufacturing in Newport, Tennessee, Shelby Williams serves hotels, restaurants, country clubs, senior living facilities, and banquet venues worldwide.
